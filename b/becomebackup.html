Become A Sponsor

  • Sponsors
  • Become A Sponsor

Becoming a sponsor has many benefits. Aside from increasing brand awareness with product placement and advertising, sponsoring PER also puts you and your company in direct contact with motivated and talented individuals, providing superior recruitment opportunities for your company.

By providing exposure to universities worldwide, the engineering community, as well as the public, PER promotes your products in a direct and open way. Ranging from our web presence, apparel, and publications, to the car itself, your company’s image can be presented along with a significant engineering accomplishment that wouldn’t otherwise exist without your support.

PER’s team members go above and beyond their core curricula; they spend the majority of their spare time honing their skills with little compensation. By the time they graduate, they will have spent thousands of hours outside of classrooms and homework becoming experienced at how real-world engineering and design is performed. This makes them opportune candidates for internships, co-op programs, and career opportunities. By sponsoring PER, you can gain direct access to these dedicated individuals, giving your company an edge in recruitment opportunities.

By sponsoring our team you are supporting students who strive to become better engineers and better professionals. You are also supporting the fact that we are a 100% emissions free racing team and we are working to help the environment by pushing electric vehicle technology further.

There are three ways to contribute to Purdue Electric Racing. Please follow the instructions HERE. If further information is needed, please contact our persident, Sandeep Baskar, at purdueelectricracing@gmail.com.
