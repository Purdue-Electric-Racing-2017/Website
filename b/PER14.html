Car Description

PER14 is the first ever electric Formula SAE vehicle produced by Purdue University. The vehicle was designed from the ground up to take full advantage of the electric powertrain. PER14 was designed to be an elegantly simple and reliable car that would represent a strong foundation for future development. The design of this vehicle was emphasizing the overall vehicle layout and systems integration to arrive at a cohesive and elegant vehicle.
